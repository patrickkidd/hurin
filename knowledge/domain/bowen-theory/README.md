# Bowen Family Systems Theory — Knowledge Base

Murray Bowen (1913–1990) developed this theory to bring the study of the human into the realm of accepted science. The theory treats the **family as the emotional unit** — not the individual — and describes eight interlocking concepts that govern human functioning across generations.

## Core Principle

The family is an emotional unit: (1) anxiety moves instantly between members, and (2) members trade "self" into the group togetherness in a fusion of selves. All eight concepts derive from this foundation.
